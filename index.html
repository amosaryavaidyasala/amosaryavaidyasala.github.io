About Us

We are a traditional Kerala Ayurvedic Clinic for complete Ayurvedic Treatment.

At Amos Arya Vaidyasala, we offer holistic Kerala Ayurvedic (Herbal) healing through a process which was developed more than 5000 years ago in India. The practice is based on the trust that health and well-being depend on a delicate balance between the mind, body & soul. We offer a number of Ayurvedic Treatments which consist of authentic Exercises, Massage Therapies & other practices.

We are a group of highly motivated, qualified and dedicated team of therapists, retreat providers & attenders to ensure the highest levels in the quality of treatment & therapeutic preparations.

NOTE: Ayurvedic Treatments are done ONLY after consultation with our Experienced Ayurvedic Graduate Doctors From Kerala Arya Vaida College
